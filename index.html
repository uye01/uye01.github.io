Hankyeol Kim김한결

M.S. student in Industrial Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Pilsung Kang at the Data Science & Business Analytics Lab.

I was trained as a physicist and now build machine-learning systems. My work so far follows one question in two directions: how models recover structure from noisy observations of the physical world, and how much we can trust what they claim to know.

That question has taken me from physics-informed neural networks and LLM confidence calibration to my current focus on video diffusion and latent reasoning in LLMs — and it points toward where I want to go next: Physical AI, models that perceive, predict, and act in the real world.
